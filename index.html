FAQ

Common questions, clear answers.

How quickly should I speak with a lawyer after an accident?

As soon as possible. Early legal guidance helps preserve evidence, protect deadlines, and avoid mistakes with insurers.

What should I bring to the first consultation?

Bring any accident reports, photos, medical records, insurance correspondence, and a timeline of key events.

Do most personal injury cases settle or go to trial?

Many cases settle, but we prepare every matter as if trial is possible to strengthen leverage and outcomes.

How will I receive updates about my case?

You receive direct updates at key milestones, with clear next steps and prompt responses to your questions.
